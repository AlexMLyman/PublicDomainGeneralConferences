First Counselor in the First Presidency 

My brethren and sisters: It becomes increasingly apparent that we need larger quarters for our General Conferences. An hour before the time of commencement this building was filled to capacity. I am sure there is the traditional ten thousand people here this morning. I hope that all will remain as quiet as possible so that everyone may hear.  
  
President Grant is "listening in" this morning.
